# Isaac Hakobian Media Kit - Design Concepts

## Concept 1: Minimalist Brutalism with Warm Accents
**Design Movement:** Brutalist Minimalism meets Luxury Tech (inspired by Hermès digital experiences, Brunello Cucinelli branding)

**Core Principles:**
- Extreme whitespace and negative space as the primary design tool
- Monochromatic typography hierarchy (black text on white, varying weights only)
- Subtle warm accent color (burnt sienna/oxblood) for CTAs and highlights
- Asymmetric grid layout with deliberate "breathing room"

**Color Philosophy:**
- **Primary:** Off-white (near-white with slight warmth, ~95% brightness)
- **Secondary:** Deep charcoal/black for text
- **Accent:** Burnt sienna (#8B4513 or similar warm brown) — reflects the bespoke, handcrafted aesthetic
- **Rationale:** This palette conveys confidence without shouting. It's the visual equivalent of a perfectly tailored suit.

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric two-column layout on desktop (content left, visual right)
- Hero section with text only (no image) — let the typography breathe
- Statistics displayed as large numerals with minimal context
- Case studies presented as vertical "cards" with extreme padding

**Signature Elements:**
1. **Thin horizontal dividers** (1px lines) separating sections — suggests precision and editorial quality
2. **Large serif typography** for headings (Playfair Display or similar) — conveys authority
3. **Subtle grain texture** on background (very faint) — adds tactile quality without distraction

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states: slight color shift (accent color appears on text)
- No animations on load — content appears instantly (confidence)
- Smooth scroll behavior with minimal easing
- Links underline on hover (classic, editorial approach)

**Animation:**
- Fade-in on scroll (very subtle, 0.3s duration)
- Hover: text color shift to accent (no scale, no bounce)
- Button hover: background color shift, no shadow

**Typography System:**
- **Display:** Playfair Display Bold (headings, 48-64px)
- **Body:** Inter Regular (body text, 16px)
- **Accent:** Inter Semibold (labels, CTAs, 14px)
- Hierarchy through size and weight, not color

**Probability:** 0.08

## Concept 2: Dark Luxury with Gold Accents (Premium Tech)
**Design Movement:** Dark Mode Luxury (inspired by high-end watch brands, Rolex digital presence, luxury SaaS)

**Core Principles:**
- Deep charcoal/near-black background (#0A0A0A)
- Gold/champagne accents for premium feel
- Generous use of transparency and layering
- Centered, symmetrical layout with card-based structure

**Color Philosophy:**
- **Primary:** Near-black background with subtle blue undertone
- **Secondary:** Warm gold (#D4AF37) for accents and highlights
- **Tertiary:** Light gray for secondary text
- **Rationale:** Gold conveys luxury and exclusivity. Dark background suggests sophistication and nighttime elegance (fits the "evening event" vibe of premium brands).

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Centered hero with large circular image (your portrait or brand mark)
- Grid-based card layout for statistics and case studies
- Floating elements with subtle shadows
- Sticky header with navigation

**Signature Elements:**
1. **Gold accent lines** (2px) framing key sections
2. **Circular image containers** with gold borders
3. **Glassmorphism cards** (semi-transparent white overlays on dark background)

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover: gold glow effect on cards
- Smooth transitions with 0.4s easing
- Micro-interactions: subtle scale-up on hover

**Animation:**
- Entrance: fade + slight upward movement (0.5s)
- Hover: gold glow shadow, slight scale (1.02x)
- Scroll: parallax effect on background elements

**Typography System:**
- **Display:** Montserrat Bold (headings, 56-72px)
- **Body:** Lato Regular (body text, 16px)
- **Accent:** Montserrat Semibold (labels, 14px)

**Probability:** 0.07

## Concept 3: Editorial Minimalism with Photography Focus
**Design Movement:** High-End Editorial Design (inspired by Kinfolk magazine, luxury lifestyle publications, Vogue digital)

**Core Principles:**
- Photography as the primary visual language
- Minimal text, maximum impact
- Full-width image sections with overlaid typography
- Serif + sans-serif pairing for editorial sophistication

**Color Philosophy:**
- **Primary:** Pure white background
- **Secondary:** Deep charcoal for text
- **Accent:** Warm gray (#8B8B8B) for secondary information
- **Rationale:** Photography drives the aesthetic. The design is a "frame" for your visual content. Neutral palette ensures images stand out.

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-width hero image with centered text overlay
- Alternating image-left/text-right and text-left/image-right sections
- Large typography overlaid directly on images
- Minimal navigation (sticky, top-right corner only)

**Signature Elements:**
1. **Full-width image sections** with 30% text overlay (semi-transparent dark background)
2. **Large pull quotes** in serif font
3. **Subtle image grain** for tactile quality

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover: image zoom (1.05x) on hover
- Text links: underline animation on hover
- Smooth scroll with image parallax

**Animation:**
- Image entrance: fade + slight zoom (0.6s)
- Hover: zoom effect on images (1.05x, 0.4s)
- Scroll: parallax effect (images move slower than text)

**Typography System:**
- **Display:** Playfair Display Bold (headings, 52-68px)
- **Body:** Inter Regular (body text, 16px)
- **Accent:** Inter Light (captions, 13px)

**Probability:** 0.09
